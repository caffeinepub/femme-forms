# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Rename the site to “femme forms” everywhere and intensify the glittery/shimmery theme while keeping the rustic sketch + silver/metallic aesthetic readable.

**Planned changes:**
- Update all user-facing brand text from “Art Shop” / “Sensual Sketches & Feminine Art” to “femme forms” across header, footer, hero, accessible labels/alt text, and page metadata (including the document title).
- Increase shimmer/glitter styling intensity consistently across key UI surfaces (header, hero, cards, and primary/outline buttons) by strengthening shimmer overlays/glows while preserving readability and usability in light and dark modes.

**User-visible outcome:** The app consistently displays the “femme forms” brand name, and the interface has a noticeably stronger glittery/shimmery look across major surfaces without layout or readability regressions.
